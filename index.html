The Recipe Archive

Stories, Recipes, and Seasonal Knowledge from America's Food Producers

The Recipe Archive is a cookbook and field guide to America's regional food traditions. Through recipes, stories, and seasonal knowledge from farmers, fishers, ranchers, gardeners, and food producers, it explores how climate, landscape, and culture shape the way we grow, cook, preserve, and share food across the United States.

The first volume is currently in development.
